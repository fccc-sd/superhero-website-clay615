  • Why I Choose Iron Man
    •

There are a few reasons I chose Iron Man as my favorite super hero. First of all, despite being a normal human, he still fights things far more powerful then him and can win. He made his first suit or armor in a cave while being held prisoner. Although he has done some impressive stuff, what makes Iron Man a good super hero isnt his strength or any super powers, but intelligence, and a willingness to risk his life to better the world. Iron Man isn't a superhero bc he has to be, he is a super hero because he wants to better the world, and use his skills to help others. On top of being a good person, he is extremely intelligent, able to make his suits form scratch, and making new and advanced technology all the time.
